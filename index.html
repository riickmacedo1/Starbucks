It's not jus coffee
It's Starbucks

In 1981, Howard Schultz (President and CEO of Starbucks) first entered a Starbucks store. From his first cup of Sumatra, Howard was attracted to a Starbucks and joined a year later. In 1983, Howard traveled to Italy and was captivated by Italian coffees and the romance of the coffee experience. He had a vision to bring the Italian coffee tradition back to the United States.
